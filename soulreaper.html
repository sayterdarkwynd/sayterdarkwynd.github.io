A 2D hybrid of RPG, Metroidvania and Adventure genres. Soul Reapers project lead hired me to required 100 maps for its three core biomes. I was solely responsible for the design and implementation of these levels as well as how progression applied in relation to various acquired player abilities. I filled additional roles where required in the short contract.



My design-related experience at Power Level Studios includes:
  • • Worked collaboratively with interns to design and impelent over 100 levels across three disctint biomes.
  • • Worked with the Creative Director to design interesting character abilities to enhance agency and provide additional options for progression.
  • • Created art assets for biome textures and abilities, and composing them into tilemaps for use in 2d level designs.
  • • Prototyped and tested gameplay features and mechanics.
