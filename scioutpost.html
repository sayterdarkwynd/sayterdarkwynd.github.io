Science Outpost

A hub world for research, shopping and quests.

Game Design Level Design Sprites Documentation

A meeting place...

The Science Outpost is a hub, where players will visit time and time again to gather new quests, engage in commerce or utilize the machines present to craft goods and learn new recipes. There are numerous sections, each dedicated to different aspects of play.
