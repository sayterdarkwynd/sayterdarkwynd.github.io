Tabletop Gaming

I adore tabletop gaming, and most of my hobby time involves playing games with my friends. I spend a great deal of time writing, mapping and planning quests, encounters and adventures, narratives and more to share through this medium.

Design for Tabletop Games

In the tabletop space, game design takes a similar shape to the digital space. Differences of course exist (such as using Theatre of the Mind over state of the art 3d graphics), but those differences diminish as traditional styles of playing adapt to the modern world. Virtual Tabletops (VTTs) have allowed the hobby to flourish online, and products like Dungeondraft and D&D Beyond make things more accessible than ever before.

Here, you'll find examples of some things I've created for this space that I have used in my own games or shared with others - or simply created for fun!
