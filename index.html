I'm a creative professional with over 5 years of industry experience in Game Design , Level Design , Documentation and Scripting. My focus is on creating engaging mechanics and gameplay that encourage player agency, exploration, and experimentation. I also have 15 years experience in Web Design , Graphic Design , Digital Art and UI/UX Design , where my projects have reached millions all over the world through print and digital media.


Videogames allow us to tell stories that truly draw the audience in, and explore themes and genres that wildly vary from the norm; I seek to enthrall players with the experiences I help to shape. My interests have always been deeply rooted in videogames and comic books, and I've spent my career developing a broad range of skills to bring such visions to life.


I also create mods in my hobby time. My major mod project (FrackinUniverse) has more than 500,000 users with a player rating of 96%! For 9 years I've acted as a Project Manager for a small team of contributors and where I have contributed Pixel Art , Level Design , Narrative Design , Scripting , lua , JSON , and more besides.
