A passionate designer devoted to bringing imagination to life, I seek professional experiences that allow my creativity to flourish and my knowledge to sharpen ever-further. My interests have always been deeply rooted in videogames and comic books, and I've spent my career developing a host of Design related skills (such as Web Design, UX, Worldbuilding, Writing, Illustration) to bring such visions to life.

For 5 years, I've worked as a Game Designer and Level Designer creating mechanics and worlds from the ground up and through rapid-iteration, bringing them to life for players. I love it; this is what I was born to do.

For 15 years prior, I worked professionally as a Web, Graphic and UX/UI Designer for numerous clients, reaching millions all over the world through print and digital media.

My current major mod project (FrackinUniverse) has more than 500,000 users worldwide! There I've acted as a Project Manager as well as creating art assets, levels, quests, scripting, writing, programming and more over the last 9 years.

I'm ready, willing and able to bring my imagination to life and design fantastic games, and together we can create something truly special.
