  • A die-hard gamer, I've been tinkering with games since I was a kid. Tabletop roleplaying, videogames, board games...anything that engages the player in a fun and meaningful way. My approach to design is oriented around this principle and plays a part in all phases of my work. I'm always seeking ways to improve both myself and the product to make the most out of every experience.

    My philosophy is simple: Make design fun. If the user isn't engaged and interested...what's the point? I'm a jack-of-all-trades, bringing many talents to the fore. If you're interested in reaching out to me for contract work contact me below.
