Hi, I'm Chris DeChamplain, a Web, Graphic and UX/UI Designer currently located in Toronto.

MY SPECIALTIES:

  • Responsive Web Design

    I've had extensive experience designing and building responsive websites and have developed an approach to my workflow that allows me to quickly tackle problems and offer viable solutions.

  • User Interfaces

    I reach the audience with friendly and intuitive interfaces. From app design to user-areas and beyond, I've got the chops to pull it off.

  • Worldbuilding

    Worldbuilding is essential to bringing digital universes to life. Designing rules, settings, cultures, scripts, levels and many other facets in games is my greatest passion.

  • Storytelling

    Bringing users engaging tales through design is among the best ways to evoke great response. Every story can be shaped to reach an audience in engaging ways!

  • Concepts

    I'm ever-curious and open-minded and always willing to apply new concepts in fresh ways. Trying new approaches is the only way to break into new territory, and I'm ready.

  • Illustration

    A lot can be conveyed with a simple sketch, or a character design. I've done quite a bit of sketching, but my real forte is in colors. I've worked on comics, digital art, game art and more besides.

13+ years experience in mobile and web UX/UI, Digital Design and extensive knowledge of dating technologies and digital marketing. A capable storyteller with big dreams and an even bigger imagination.

A jack-of-all-trades, I've developed a wide array of skills over the years including Responsive Design and Digital Marketing, HTML, CSS, UX/UI, Adobe CS, Wordpress, Wireframing/Storyboarding, Concept/Prototyping, Sound and Video Editing, Spriting, Illustration, Inking, Colorist, Art Direction, Team Management, Lua programming and JSON scripting.

A die-hard gamer, I've dreams of taking the industry by storm. My current project (FrackinUniverse) has 200,000 players worldwide! There I've led a team in creating art assets, level designs, scripting, writing, programming and more over the last several years. As a result, it has grown into one of the largest and most comprehensive overhauls ever released for a game!
