Designing for Hunter's Moon

What is Hunter's Moon?

A deckbuilder with a focus on rich narrative and story elements over fail-and-repeat gameplay, Hunter's Moon provides something different in the genre. We had a very short development window on this project (6 months from start to finish,) and had purchased rights to the code from Menace From the Deep to provide the backbone of our project.

The Art Director had a very particular art style that needed to be emulated across the entire UI to provide a cohesive theme and mood, and the result is a steampunk adventure with a unique approach to the genre.
