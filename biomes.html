Bee Hive

Threat Level: Average

Description: Giant bees horrify and amaze in this sub-biome. They are found within most forested or jungle type biomes. They provide a good boost to a few resources, most notably amber.

Common Resources: Honey, Amber, Hardened Carapace

Known Threats: Giant Bees, Slowing (Debuff - Honey), Poison (Debuff - Bee Stings)

Bog

Threat Level: Low

Description: Thick and mucky bogs make for slow movement at times, but the sheer abundance of natural resources such as algae and bone make it a desirable destination for access to biofuels and interesting genetic discoveries.

Common Resources: Bone, Algae, Swampwater

Known Threats: Slowing (Mud), Slowing (Water)

Chromatic

Threat Level: High

Description: Deadly radiation bathes these planets, making gathering their valuable resources rather fatal without the appropriate protective gear. It's well worth the trip, due to an abundance of Prisilite.

Common Resources: Prisilite, Moonstone

Known Threats: Radiation, Radiation Burns

Frozen Volcanic

Threat Level: High

Description: You'll find plenty of valuable resources on these deadly and seemingly impossible worlds. Somehow, lava and ice exist in harmony in all defiance of thermodynamics and reality. Trianglium is common here, along with mineral deposits of high value.

Common Resources: Cinnabar, Prisilite, Trianglium, Crystal, Diamond

Known Threats: Liquid Nitrogen, Freezing, Ice, Slush, Snow, Lava, Burning Rock, Deadly Storms

Infernus

Threat Level: Extreme

Description: There are no worlds hotter than Infernus capable of housing any sort of survivable exposure. Lava, deadly storms and volatile creatures threaten your life at every turn. The very atmosphere will burn to death anyone unwise enough to come unprotected. Despite the risks, the rewards are exceptional.

Common Resources: Cinnabar, Densinium, Black Ash, Magma Rock, Burning Magma Rock, Scorching Magma Rock

Known Threats: Extreme Heat, Lava, Deadly Storms, Molten Metal, Deadly Fauna
