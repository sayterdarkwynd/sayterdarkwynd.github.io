Designing for Thrive

What is Thrive?

As Lead Game Designer on this ambitious city-builder, I drew on my expertise in UX/UI Design to create the visual language for the games interface as desired by the Game Director and other stakeholders, designed the FTUE experience for new players, and conceptualized and implemented all interface art. I was also responsible for the designing and documentation of game mechanics, features, systems and pitches.

What is Dual Universe?

Dual Universe, a product of Novaquark, is a spacefaring MMO centered on social systems, market simulation, building, and complex physics. These aspects all combine to produce a truly unique experience that blends the creativity of Minecraft with the depth of Eve Online.

Level Design on this project was largely centered around the First-Time user experience, with a focus on tutorials and gameplay guidance. Careful planning, thorough documentation and rapid prototyping were all necessary to ensure the highest quality of added content. Puzzles and events were a part of this as well.
