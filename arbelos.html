  • With Arbelos Interactive, I've had all my skillset challenged in some way. This has been an interesting journey, with numerous projects on the go simultaneously. My primary duties here are in regard to an educational game called Stemuli: The pandemic of 2020 changed the way many things were done, and that included school. Through this program Students and Educators can meet online and engage in conventional schooling complete with grades, but with a heavy focus on STEM topics. In addition to education, students are also able to play in and interfact with the game world around them in meaningful ways.

    Level designs in Stemuli were treated as exploration zones allowing students to wander in large regions with plenty of places to hide, seek, interact and roleplay with one another. Rather than focusing on conventional challenges like platforming or combat students instead were presented with an array of minigames to fullfill these objectives while presenting them with learning opportunities in the form of tests, teamwork-based excercises, creativity, and leaving the bulk of their non-educational gametime to instead be self-driven play.
